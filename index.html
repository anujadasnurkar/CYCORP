ABOUT US

CYCORP is Co-founded by Milind Changani, Yomesh Rao and Jasleen Changani - leading professionals in the field of architectural design and liaison for redevelopment and development across Mumbai. Senior partners include Danish Fakih who has been in the field of hospitality and architectural design. With over 250+ ongoing projects, the Firm is One Stop Consultancy and Liaising Firm Comprising of Engineers, Valuer’s, Lawyers, Architects and Project management Consultants with one Common aim, to provide unparalleled, comprehensive Professional services to Clients which include developers, institutions, corporates, Society who want to carry out self-redevelopment. The Group has currently 3 subsidiary companies: YMSCL, BNSA, Studio C. Integrity and Transparency are our founding principles. We constantly strive towards excellence and welcome feedback from our clients to help improve our services. We value each and every one of our clients and understand their individual needs and circumstances.
